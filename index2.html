Formation of Srama Shakthi Sanghas

Jeevana Sravanthi Drought Association is acting a key role in monitoring and awareness concept in Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act. Since September 2014 JSDA formed groups of Job Card Holders and formed them in Srama Sakthi Sanghas (SSS) which in turn are confederated into Srama Shakthi Sangha Federations in Bukkapatnam Mandal. This confederation is aimed to ensure their rights to get 100 days of assured wage employment as assured in the Act. These federations act a key role in future and to be act as an independent body to get their benefits and Rights by themselves. JSDA is involved in giving them trainings at regular intervals, take the issues to the concerned authorities through mobilizing them to put them in the form of complaints / suggestions.

Puttaparthi is a great historical and spiritual place. Bhagavan Sri Sathya Sai Baba preaches that “Cleanliness is next to the Godliness“. So we are supporting to the Nagara Panchayath to clean the streets of Puttaparthi. We have promoted a few volunteers who clean and collect the waste from several identified places in Puttaparthi deposit the waste in dust-bins which is later transported to the waste yard. Thus, the organization is involving the communities in the sanitation aspects.
